Our story

A home near the Lord's doorstep

Sri Sai Srinivasa Homestay is a family-owned homestay dedicated to providing pilgrims and travelers with the warmth and comfort of a real home—something hotels often cannot offer. We provide clean, well-maintained 1 BHK and 2 BHK accommodations at some of the best prices in Tirupati. Our family welcomes every guest with genuine hospitality, ensuring a comfortable, affordable, and memorable stay. Feel Like Home is not just our tagline—it's our promise.

Founder & Host

BV Sai

I'm BV Sai, founder of Sri Sai Srinivasa Homestay. For over 4 years, my family and I have had the privilege of welcoming more than 3,000 guests from across India and around the world. Our goal has always been simple: to give pilgrims and traveling families something that a typical hotel often cannot—a warm, personal, and truly homely experience.

Having grown up in Tirupati, I understand the needs of visitors coming for Lord Venkateswara's darshan, family vacations, and business trips. That's why we created our 1 BHK and 2 BHK homestays to offer the comfort, cleanliness, and peace of mind that we would want for our own family. Together with my mother, we personally manage every aspect of the homestay—from housekeeping and cleanliness to maintaining the air conditioning, amenities, and overall guest experience. We believe that every guest deserves a spotless, comfortable, and welcoming place to stay.

— BV Sai
